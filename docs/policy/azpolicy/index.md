# Azure Policy

Azure Policy is a powerful tool offered by Microsoft. It enables organizations to manage security policies for their Azure resources. It also ensures compliance with organizational standards, regulations, and best practices.

By enforcing compliance, preventing misconfiguration, and enhancing security, these policies assist governance by defining and assigning rules to our Azure resources.

## Benefits

- Azure Policy integrates with Azure Resource Manager to apply policies to resources at creation, ensuring compliance from the start.
- Provides real-time compliance monitoring and reporting, enabling security teams to easily identify and address security compliance issues.
- Offers a rich set of built-in policies and initiatives covering a wide range of compliance scenarios.
- Help subscription owners and their teams to move quickly without worry of breaking security compliance.
